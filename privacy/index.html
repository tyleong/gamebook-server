✦ Privacy Policy ✦

Effective date: 7 June 2026 · Version 1.0
Ember Crown (and future titles in the Gamebook Adventures series) are interactive fiction games published for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights. We are committed to keeping your experience private and secure.
📋

1. Information We Collect

Gamebook Adventures titles are text-based games with no account, login, or profile required. We do not ask for your name, date of birth, or any personal details to play.

The only data collected is through third-party services embedded in the app:

Service Data Collected Purpose Shared With
Google AdMob Advertising ID, device info, IP address Show ads (free version) Google
Google Play Billing Purchase receipt Verify in-app purchase (remove ads) Google
Google Play Console Crash reports, app version App stability monitoring Google
Game progress (endings found, choices made) is stored on your device only using browser localStorage. It is never transmitted to us or any server.
🎮

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Ads — If you use the free version, AdMob may serve personalised or non-personalised ads based on your device's advertising settings. You can opt out in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.
  • In-app purchase — If you purchase "Remove Ads", Google Play processes the transaction. We receive only a confirmation token to unlock the ad-free experience. No payment details are ever visible to us.
  • Crash data — Google Play Console automatically collects crash reports to help us fix bugs. This data is anonymised and aggregated.

We do not use any data for profiling, marketing, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

🔒

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

The third-party services involved are:

  • Google AdMob — advertising. Google's Privacy Policy applies.
  • Google Play — app distribution and billing. Google's Privacy Policy applies.

We operate no backend server. No data is sent to us directly.

🗓️

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Game progress stored on your device can be deleted at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data: Settings → Apps → Ember Crown → Clear Data, or
  • Uninstalling the app.

We do not retain any personal data on our own servers because we do not operate any servers for this app.

👶

5. Children's Privacy

Gamebook Adventures titles are rated for users aged 12 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 is using the app and you have concerns, please contact us using the details below.

🛡️

6. Security

  • All game progress data is stored locally on your device.
  • Ad serving and billing use Google's infrastructure with industry-standard encryption.
  • We do not operate servers that store your personal information.
⚖️

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it.

  • For data stored on your device, you can exercise these rights directly by clearing app data or uninstalling.
  • For data held by Google, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and your Google account settings.
📝

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

This policy covers all titles published under the Gamebook Adventures series by the same developer.
